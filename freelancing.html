Services

Additionally to my main Job ( CV ) I offer a range of services to meet your embedded systems needs, including firmware development for different platforms and systems, consulting on best practices like repeatable builds, unit testing, hardware in the loop and more.

My main expertise are in developing firmware for constrained IoT devices, energy harvesting or battery powered. I can offer to provide a development using either

  • Short range radio protocols like: Zigbee, Thread, BLE or enocean
  • Long range: Lora
  • Cellular: LTE-M / NB-IoT
Additionally I can support in setting up in either technologies to gather the generated data or on the device processing of the data using Tiny-ML technologies.
